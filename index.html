F1 Tyre Degradation Simulator

CS50 Final Project. Program loads stint data from the FastF1 package and cleans the data (e.g. removes Safety Car laps). Models tyre compound performance and effects of fuel & temperature, to ultimately devise the most optimal race strategy across different circuits.

Biomimicry in aircraft wing design

Research paper investigating and comparing radical wing concepts aimed at meeting Net Zero targets. Ran each design through MicroCFD to record L/D ratios for quantitative analysis. Submitted to the Sustainable Skies World Summit and Global Innovators' Challenge 2026.

ESA SkyMed Demonstration Project

Designed an ESA-approved handling system (scissor lift) in Fusion to carry NHS payloads between hospital roofs using BVLOS drones. Selected suitable materials (e.g. lift platforms used CFPR honeycomb sandwich).
