Constructed a mini wooden glider. Chose light & stiff materials (e.g. foam board for wings, wooden dowel for fuselage). Maximised glide ratio by adding camber and dihedral. Model was initially tail-heavy, so improved stability by adding pennies (counterweights) to the nose.
