Biomimicry in aircraft wing design

Research paper investigating and comparing radical wing concepts aimed at meeting Net Zero targets. Ran each design through MicroCFD to record L/D ratios for quantitative analysis. Submitted to the Sustainable Skies World Summit and Global Innovators' Challenge 2026.

ESA SkyMed Demonstration Project

Designed an ESA-approved handling system (scissor lift) in Fusion to carry NHS payloads between hospital roofs using BVLOS drones. Selected suitable materials (e.g. lift platforms used CFPR honeycomb sandwich for its chemical resistance and stiffness properties).

Fusion (CAD) Simulation Materials

Model Balsa Glider

Constructed a mini wooden glider. Chose light & stiff materials (e.g. foam board for wings, wooden dowel for fuselage). Maximised glide ratio by adding camber and dihedral. Model was initially tail-heavy, so improved stability by adding pennies (counterweights) to the nose.

Principles of Flight Aerodynamics Materials

Air Traffic Control (ATC) Simulator

2D automated ATC landing system built following an adaptive waterfall lifecycle. Conducted 2 stakeholder interviews and 6 surveys to obtain user requirements. Delivered 700+ lines of Python code and 200+ pages of documentation, meeting all 12 requirements.

ESA CanSat Competition

Built an electronic payload launched into the atmosphere to collect environmental data. Python-controlled BMP280 sensor and Raspberry Pi. Coded a closed-loop feedback system to operate servos that opened/closed landing flaps. Industrial Cadets silver award.

Autonomous Payload Robot (Leonardo)

Obstacle-navigating robot built during Leonardo work experience. Coded the robot's autonomous payload-handling functions in Python with Raspberry Pi. Reduced the weight of payload-carrying claws by 70% over several iterations in Fusion.
